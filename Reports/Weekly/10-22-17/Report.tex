Weeks 1 and 2: scheduling, review of the previous codes (8 hours)
Week 3: final scheduling decision
Week 4: project design (5-15 hours)
Weeks 5, 6 and 7: coding (10h/week)
Week 8: debugging
Week 9: optimization / debugging
Week 10: report / presentation / user manual / oral presentation

Concepts of the project:
1) Acquisition of the image with the Kinect RGB-D sensor (range 0,5 - 4,5 m) - returns image with resolution 424 x 512
2) Background subtraction (depth subtraction) to get the region of interest
3) Point Cloud formation
4) Denoising
5) Triangulation
6) Colorizing
